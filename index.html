I am a Scientist at TCS Research and Innovation. Before this, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at IIT Kharagpur. I am interested in natural language processing and understanding using machine learning and deep learning approaches. Recently, I have worked on aspect-sentiment extraction, information extraction from material science research articles, causality extraction, and question-answering. I did my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore. My doctoral thesis is on relation extraction using deep neural networks from the text for the enrichment of knowledge bases. I also have the interest to work in machine translation, machine reading comprehension, knowledge-base completion tasks.

Before joining the National University of Singapore, I did my bachelor's and masters in Computer Science & Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India. I also worked as a software engineer at Hewlett-Packard India and PwC India for 5 years.
