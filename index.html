I am postdoctoral researcher at IIT Kharagpur, India. I am interested in natural language processing and understanding using machine learning and deep learning approaches. Recently, I have worked on aspect-sentiment extraction, information extraction from material science research articles, causality extraction, and question-answering. I did my PhD at the department of Computer Science, National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof Hwee Tou Ng. My doctoral thesis is on relation extraction using deep neural networks from the text for the enrichment of knowledge bases. I also have interest to work on machine translation, machine reading comprehension, knowledge-base completion tasks.
